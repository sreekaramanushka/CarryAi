Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CarryAI context continuation and privacy policies.

What is CarryAI and how does it work? ▼

CarryAI is a lightweight, local-first Chrome extension that helps you continue coding sessions across multiple AI chatbots (like Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek, and Grok). It reads the active chat log, compiles key goals, decisions, and tasks into a context capsule, and pre-fills the prompt area on target AI tabs automatically when they load.

Is my conversation data safe? ▼

Yes, 100% safe. CarryAI processes everything locally inside your browser. There are no remote database servers, user tracking metrics, or cloud analytics APIs. Your data is stored on-device in Chrome local storage, ensuring absolute privacy for your proprietary codebase details.

Can the target AI access files uploaded to the previous chat? ▼

No. Browser sandboxing restrictions strictly prevent chrome extensions from reading files on your local drive or uploading binary contents programmatically into other tabs. Because of this, CarryAI automatically includes a warning checklist of all attachments in the restored context prompt.

⚠ File Upload Checklist Checklist: CarryAI warns: "User, please re-upload these files to this new chat tab if the AI needs to read their contents." This ensures the target model is aware of the files, and you have a quick checklist of files to drag-and-drop into the input area.
Which AI chatbots does CarryAI support? ▼

CarryAI has native parser adapters supporting: ChatGPT (chatgpt.com), Claude (claude.ai), Gemini (gemini.google.com), DeepSeek (deepseek.com), Grok (grok.com), Poe (poe.com), and Perplexity (perplexity.ai).
